About Us

Greg Beauregard, President of Pacer Service Center started at Pacer as a part time co-op student from Mona Shores. In the 1990’s Greg and his Wife Victoria became sole owners of the Corporation. Greg has over 30 years experience in vehicle repairs, along with as many years of experience in the Propane Industries from transportation to an unsurpassed knowledge of the workings and regulations on propane transportation and bulk delivery. Pacer Service Center is the only authorized Caterpillar parts and services dealer in West Michigan. Pacer Service Center is also a licensed repair facility by the State of Michigan and all technicians are licensed diesel repairmen.
